Ambition project

This is a game I made completely by myself
with C# with unity in the second semester of previous schoolyear,
the assignment was to create something of what your ambition is.
For me its building games,
so I started to think about what kind of game I wanted to build,
I didn’t have that much time so kept it simple and made a 2D endless runner.
The game is fully complete so it includes a home screen, background music etc.
I also made the visuals of the player myself.
I learned that the most important thing when building a game
is to always have a good planning
for everything you want to do when you have limited time
because fast enough I discovered
that I had to do a lot of things in the small amount of time I had
because I didn’t have a good planning
